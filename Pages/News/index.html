What Happens to Broken News

The newly founded town, will it recover?

Always on the front page of a newspaper is the outstanding or breaking news. Now, after the news has finished breaking, what does it do? Where does it go? Researchers have been working on this for maybe two months and they are finally getting results. Using some shifty tactics, none of which were released to the press, they managed to make a breaking news article about a researcher, and send him to the place where broken news goes. By some other even more shifty tactics they summoned him back to this universe. Apart from only speaking in poetry, he seems to be fine. So far what we have gathered from him is this:

A missing book,

Things you said had gone away,
Whisked off into space,
And others laughed to your dismay,
But worry not,
For you were right,
They were taken in the night,
Taken to a far-away land,
Hidden from the sight of man,
It is called, if you must know,
The Place where Lost Things Go,
So if there’s something you can’t find,

It has gone to the hidden place,
The Place Where Lost Things Go.
