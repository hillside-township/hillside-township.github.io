Hillside Village and Blank Nation Peace Treaty

Hillside Village Township and Country and Blank Nation

The Bamboo Pact

The Twelfth  of February 2020 C. E.

Overview

This is the Peace Treaty of Hillside Village Township and Blank Nation.  The threat of war has loomed over our shoulders and relationships have been very tense.  These guidelines are mandatory for all citizens or members of the above-mentioned groups. If anyone disagrees, they will not be allowed inside of the other nation, nor be able to establish trade or contact.

“ Let us hope for a good today, and a better tomorrow. ”-  Mayor Justin McCubbin, 2020

“With this treaty comes hope of a time of peace and advancement.”Second in Command, Sweeper, Construction Worker, Herbalist Hamza Nasher, 2020

Rules

  1. Trade :  Fair trade shall be established between the two nations of Hillside and Blank for the benefit of both.
  2. Secrecy and Security:  The Blank Nation wishes to not have its location be known to all, and shall not be sought out.  If this location is found accidentally, word of it shall not be spread and you are expected to keep this knowledge a secret.  Likewise, all properties in Hillside except for the HCC  shall be regarded as private property to the members of Blank Nation and shall not be entered into unless invited.  The town of Hillside itself shall not be entered without permission of a citizen.
  3. Threats:  Threats to both nations shall be dealt with at a public meeting in which the threat is to be neutralized and controlled.
  4. Theft:  There shall be no stealing of property between the two nations. If anything is stolen, it shall break the truce.
  5. Materials   : Materials from the large stick pile, the bamboo pile in front of the chickens, and behind the woodshop belong to both nations and shall be shared somewhat nicely.
  6. Entrance: All visitors shall not enter without consulting the leader(s) of each civilization. You may also be invited to enter the civilization .  You may not disrupt any progress made by any sides, along with no breaking the rules of that civilization.  In order to enter, you must also read the rules and guidelines, if need be, of the  certain civilization. There shall be no interruptions of the day to day activities of each nation.
  7. DMZ: The Demilitarized Zone is behind the stick pile and is where a meeting of the two nations can take place.  Meetings should start here before going into another nation unprovoked just to tell someone something.  Doing so can be a sign of aggression and may break the pact.
  8. Punishment:  If someone does not follow the above rules, they shall spend one day in service of the the other nation.

Specifications

This pact shall bring rest to the tensions between the two nations. It will keep peace hopefully for many years. Anything against the pact will break the fragile treaty, which shall be printed in triplicate, one copy for each nation and one for being in triplicate which will be kept in the DMZ. When one leader is gone, the pact must be resigned by both leaders.

Goals

  1. Establishing Trade

The Blank nation is allowed to participate in the market day, renting plots like Hillside citizens. This shall bring new items into the trade cycle.

  1. No Interference

The two nations cannot interfere with government activities, such as elections or private meetings.  If there is a public activity, members of the Blank Nation or other unaffiliated people may join in if the activity allows.

  1. Governments Have Secrets

All governments may keep secrets from each other, as long as they are not plans involving disruption or war against each other. All Governments are entitled to keep these secrets, and have the right not to tell anyone. If you are caught spying or trying to intervene with these secrets, you will be punished accordingly. Governments have the right not to tell anyone, and are not forced to.
